How to make an pretty website

1. Use colorhunt.co to find really pretty color pallets. ( Make sure to use monotone , and light colors )

2. Have formatting that fits your style! ( Your choice of formatting , but make it pleasing to look at )

3. Pick a good font! ( Examples : Monotone , Verdana , Cursive , ect )

4. Have fun! ( You don't have to follow my advice but I just like this style of making a website!)

* Also, feel free to adjust the colors however you'd like ^V^ *
