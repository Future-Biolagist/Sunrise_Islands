Arts


What arts do they have in Sunrise Islands ?

The different types of art include. music , painting , pottery, carving, and weaving
All the musical instruments are made of a tough plant fiber , and the art
is made on thin flower petals. Carvings and pottery are made on/ with thick plant stems
Beautiful weavings are made with soft spider silk.


Who makes art in Sunrise Islands?

Star weavers (or spiders) make the art , using their soft spider silk
and many legs to create masterpieces. A star weavers music is wonderful
to listen to when you are not feeling your best and that is why the monarch
has a Royal Star weaver.
